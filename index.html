About

Hi, I'm Ekta – an Architect, Graphic Designer and Ornithology Enthusiast.

I am passionate about exploring Built Environments through the lens of intricate connections between the socio-cultural-economic and ecological fabric that shapes human settlement. After my undergraduate studies in Bachelor of Architecture from CEPT University (India) and student exchange at TU Delft (The Netherlands), I worked with the Ladakh Ecological Development Group, an NGO based in Leh (high altitude Himalayas).

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Landscape Architecture at National University of Singapore (NUS). I am also a registered Architect at Council of Architecture (COA, India).
