About

Hi, I'm Ekta – an Architect, Graphic Designer and Ornithology Enthusiast.

I am passionate about exploring Built Environments through the lens of intricate connections between the socio-cultural-economic fabric that shapes human life in Urban and Rural areas. My passion for it led to work opportunities across various parts of India, Switzerland, Netherland and Singapore

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Landscape Architecture at National University of Singapore (NUS). I am also a registered Architect at Council of Architecture (COA, India) and Singapore Institute of Landscape Architects (Student member, SILA).
